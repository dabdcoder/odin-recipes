Zoro's Three-Sword Skewers

Ingredients

  • Chicken or beef slices
  • Bell peppers
  • Red onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Wooden skewers

Preparation

Marinate meat slices in soy sauce and garlic powder. Thread them onto skewers alternating with bell peppers and red onions. Grill or bake until cooked through.
